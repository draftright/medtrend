About Us

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Medtrend offer?

Medtrend provides digital marketing, drug procurement, specialist doctor outsourcing, medical supplies and equipment procurement, and medical tourism services.

How can I schedule an appointment?

You can easily schedule an appointment through our online booking system or by contacting our office directly.

Do you offer international healthcare services?

Yes, we specialize in medical tourism, providing tailored healthcare solutions for international patients seeking world-class care.

Can you help with medical equipment procurement?

Absolutely! We offer a wide range of new and refurbished medical equipment for diagnostic centers and healthcare facilities.

How do I get in touch with your healthcare consultants?
